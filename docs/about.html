Retrieval-Augmented Clinical Decision Support: A Hybrid Structured-Text Fusion Framework

Predicting 30-Day Hospital Readmission from MIMIC-III

This system predicts the likelihood of a patient being readmitted to the hospital within 30 days of discharge. It combines structured clinical data (diagnoses, procedures, lab results) with insights extracted from physician discharge notes using natural language processing. The result is a risk score and plain-language summary designed to help clinical managers prioritize follow-up care.
